Thoughts on Reason

Reason is the bedrock of democracy the bedrock of human nature. It derives the qualities we most cherish. But above all, its the one aspect of ourselves we truly own.

Reason is built on information, and when our information is corrupted, not even reason can save us.

When I think of reason, I think of the stoics and their love of reason best encapsulated by another fellow, a Mr. Boethius

But I also think of man known to critique pure reason...yes, A Mr. Emmanuelle Kant.

My thoughts on reason lie on many dimensions, and questions.

I wonder what the relation is between this concept of reason to consciousness I wonder how intrinsic reason is to our humanity? I wonder what it means for other beings to encapsulate reason? I wonder how we seem to assume that reason manifests in the same way for all beings? I wonder why reason has been the backbone for all thought. I wonder what substitute exists for reason?
