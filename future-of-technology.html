The Future of Tech Post-Pandemic

Pandemic has solidified the need for "robust telecommunications"prioritizing: * Security * Strength * Prosperity

Everything needs a Remote Option

Business: Video Communication and Collaboration is the new status quo

Learning: Learning Online is the Norm, and the option likely won't be going away

Healthcare: Telehealth is rapidly accelerating too.

Industry: Everything needs to be automated.

Some of the pandemic;s biggest business ipacts have been in supply chain management and asset tracking.

Entertainment has completely changed. Hollywood is Digital, not the box office. And the bars, concerts etc. have changed to video too.

"The Remote X world": In a nutshell: Everyone should get fast reliable network connections. The cloud will play a vital part in this world.

IIOT (Internet of things) will even change the workplace. Trackers, detectors, etc. will be used to make sure everyone isn't sick.

(Security will become even better as a result too.)

Future of Innovation:

Our post-pandemic world will require new types of technology that can facilitate creativity and augment human intelligence.

Cobots will be available online to help automate as much as possible.

Innovation will test the balance of security (health-based) and privacy (chinese human trackers used in Covid)

5G will be taken full advantage of.

Questions are: * What will change as a result of 5G (aside from self-driving of course) * What new careers will emerge? * What careers will go away? * What's going to be invented? * How is the government going to get involved?
